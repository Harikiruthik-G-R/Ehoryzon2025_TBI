Intro to Incubation

    What is an Incubator?

Technology Business Incubation is a dynamic process of business enterprise development. Our incubator nurtures young firms, helping them to survive and grow during the startup period when they are most vulnerable. It speeds up the commercialization of R&D outputs and creates additional avenues of employment.

    TBI@KEC

  • A pioneering joint venture between Kongu Engineering College and Department of Science and Technology, Government of India
  • A prestigious ₹44 million project
  • First of its kind in India
  • A golden opportunity for entrepreneur aspirants to transform innovative ideas into viable products in emerging technology areas

Our Vision & Mission

Vision

  • To create world-class entrepreneurs in emerging technology areas
  • To adapt to the rapidly changing environment and make significant contributions to the nation's growth

Mission

  • To provide a congenial atmosphere with state-of-the-art infrastructure that encourages entrepreneurs to undertake business development in emerging technologies
  • To deliver essential support services needed for sustainable growth of newly developed entrepreneurs
