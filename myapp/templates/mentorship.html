About Our Mentorship Program

Technology business incubation at KEC is more than just providing space and facilitiesâ€”it's about comprehensive mentorship that nurtures young entrepreneurs through their most vulnerable startup period. The TBI mentorship program at Kongu Engineering College is designed to accelerate the commercialization of R&D outputs and create additional avenues of employment.

As a joint venture between Kongu Engineering College and the Department of Science and Technology, Government of India, our Rs.44 million project stands as the first of its kind in India. The mentorship initiative offers a golden opportunity for entrepreneur aspirants to convert their innovative ideas into tangible products in emerging technology areas with guidance from industry experts and academic professionals.

Key Mentorship Activities

Technology Enterprises

Promoting new technology based enterprises through innovation support and incubation

Entrepreneur Support

Providing tailored services to new as well as existing entrepreneurs

Commercialization

Helping startups commercialize their developed technologies

Business Basics

Orientation towards business management and help in preparation of business plans

Consultancy

Technical, legal, and financial consulting services for startup growth

Technology Events

Organizing technology shows, clinics, and trade fairs for networking

Training & HRD

Short-term courses on Human Resources Development for entrepreneurs

IPR Support

Technology support, quality assurance, marketing, and Intellectual Property Rights guidance

Legal & Finance

Assistance in arranging licenses, finance, clearances and legal help

Our Vision & Mission

Our Vision

  • To create Entrepreneurs of world class quality in the areas of emerging technologies
  • To cater to the needs of changing environment and contribute towards Nation's growth

Our Mission

  • To provide congenial atmosphere with adequate infrastructure facilities for encouraging entrepreneurs to undertake business development in the emerging technologies.
  • To provide the support service needed for sustainable growth of the newly developed entrepreneurs.

The mentorship program at TBI KEC is dedicated to transforming innovative ideas into successful businesses through expert guidance, comprehensive support, and a nurturing ecosystem.
